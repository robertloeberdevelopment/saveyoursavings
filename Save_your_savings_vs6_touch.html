SAVE YOUR SAVINGS! - A fun HTML5 game - Prevent bankers from escaping with bonuses taken from your savings

Puncture the inflated bankers with your needle to prevent them from escaping the bank with the bonuses they took from your savings. The higher the bonuses they carry, the faster they run! If you hit 10 bankers in a row, you earn $ 15.000,- bonus yourself, so you can reverse your losses in profit. Move the needle with the mouse to hit the escaping bankers.

If you are mad about the financial crisis, and you blame the bankers for it, this HTML5 game is a nice form of anger management. Are you able to stop the running bankers?
